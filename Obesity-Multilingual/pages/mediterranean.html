Eat for Life, the Mediterranean Way

Discover a heart-healthy way of eating inspired by the sun-drenched coasts of the Mediterranean. It's not a strict diet, but a joyful lifestyle built on fresh, flavorful foods.

The Core of the Diet

Let's look closer at the key components that make this eating pattern so powerful.

Embrace Healthy Fats

Olive oil is the primary source of fat, replacing butter and margarine. Avocados, nuts, and seeds are also key, providing heart-healthy monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats.

Eat a Rainbow of Produce

Vegetables and fruits are the foundation of most meals. Aim for a wide variety to get a full spectrum of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Focus on Fish

Eat fish and seafood, especially fatty fish like salmon, mackerel, and sardines, at least twice a week. They are rich in omega-3 fatty acids.

Choose Whole Grains

Select whole grains like oats, brown rice, quinoa, and whole-wheat bread and pasta over refined grains for more fiber and nutrients.

Rethink Your Protein

Enjoy poultry, eggs, and dairy (like cheese and yogurt) in moderation. Red and processed meats are eaten only occasionally.

Flavor with Herbs & Spices

Use generous amounts of herbs and spices like garlic, oregano, rosemary, and basil to flavor food instead of relying on salt.
